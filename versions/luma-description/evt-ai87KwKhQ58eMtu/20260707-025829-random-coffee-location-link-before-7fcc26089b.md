Missed someone at your poster session? Want another chance to explain the idea, ask better questions, or hear the poster from the person who made it?

Revenge Your Poster Session is a casual Cafe @ICML slot for second chances: presenters can give a lightweight repeat of their poster, and everyone else can sit in, listen, ask, and connect.

No registration questions. Choose **Just Listening** if you want to attend, or **Present My Poster** if you want to give your poster another run.
